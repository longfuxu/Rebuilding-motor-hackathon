What was measured

Per sampled frame (Cα): total radius of gyration, whole-ring RMSD and per-subunit RMSD to a folded reference structure, and per-subunit Rg (the 1750 aa split into its 5 covalent copies). Reference structures: the folded design used for MD, native apo, and the 7JQQ helical state. Metrics below; the divergence from the fold-in-context predictors and MD is recorded as an observation, not resolved here.

BioEmu samples approximate draws from the equilibrium (Boltzmann) distribution, so these ten stand in for the sampled distribution. All ten have total Rg 5.9–8.6 nm.

Observations (descriptive)

  • The gp16 single-domain control (327 aa) samples are compact (Rg 2.29 nm) with no chain breaks.
  • The cp233 samples have larger total Rg than the three reference structures, whole-ring RMSD 45–76 Å to the folded design, and per-subunit RMSD 9–28 Å; per-subunit Rg is mostly 2.3–2.9 nm.
  • These cp233 samples differ from the fold-in-context predictions (AF3 + Boltz + OF3, previously scored closed 5/5) and from the unbiased OpenMM MD (which stayed closed). This divergence is recorded as an observation and is not resolved here.

Not yet done · open questions

  • Only 10 samples were drawn — convergence / coverage of the equilibrium distribution was not assessed.
  • BioEmu's physical_steering denoiser config was not used; a 2-repeat sub-construct, conditioning/initializing on the AF3/Boltz folded ring, a paired (non-block-diagonal) MSA, longer sampling, and other seeds were not tried.
  • Whether the block-diagonal tiled MSA supplies enough inter-subunit signal for BioEmu was not tested.
  • No independent ensemble method or wet-lab measurement has been run to adjudicate the divergence.

Separately available context (not a conclusion about this run): cp233 was previously scored closed by AF3+Boltz+OF3 (5/5), US-aligns to the native gp16 fold (TM 0.94), and stayed closed in unbiased OpenMM MD; those results live in outputs/ and md/openmm_validation/.
